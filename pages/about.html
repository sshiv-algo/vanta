OUR STORY

Why anonymity matters

We built Misto because the internet lost something important: honesty. When every post is tied to your name, your employer, and your follower count — you stop saying what you actually mean.

We wanted to create a space where a 16-year-old in Jakarta and a 30-year-old in Berlin could share the same vulnerability, and neither would know or care who the other was.

Misto is that space. Anonymous by default, empathetic by design.

Our Mission

To give every person on the planet a safe, anonymous space to express themselves — free from identity pressure.

Our Vision

A world where your ideas are valued for what they say — not who said them.
